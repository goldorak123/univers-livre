Frequently Asked Questions

Encounters by Moonlight
How do I install the app (PWA) on my phone?
To activate the Progressive Web App for free and experience full-screen immersion without a navigation bar, simply click the associated button at the bottom of the page in the PWA section. For a complete experience, we recommend installing both of our sites as a PWA: simonlero.com and mon-univers.ca.
Where can I buy the book?
"Encounters by Moonlight" is available on all major platforms: Amazon (Kindle and Paperback), Apple Books, Kobo, Shopify, Payhip (for ePub and the exclusive PDF version) and Google Play. You can also order it via the Ingram network, directly at simonlero.com or see the full list of our distribution partners.
Is the book available in paperback?
Absolutely. You can order the premium paperback edition on Amazon, Shopify, or via Lulu.com. Print-on-demand ensures impeccable quality and fast delivery.
What are the novel's themes?
The novel is a poetic immersion through music, a psychological and LGBT romance, also exploring nostalgia, self-discovery through art, and the melancholy beauty of passing time.
In which languages is the book available?
The novel is currently available in French ("Rencontres au Clair de Lune"), English ("Encounters by Moonlight"), and Spanish ("Encuentros a la Luz de la Luna").
Why the title "Encounters by Moonlight"?
The title evokes the suspended moment where truth is revealed, away from the day's hustle. It is a metaphor for inner clarity, intimately linked to the atmosphere of Beethoven's famous Moonlight Sonata. In this universe, everything is connected.
Is it a series or a standalone novel?
This novel is designed as a complete and independent work.
Who is Simon Lero?
Simon Lero is a Quebec author who seeks, through his words, to make music speak, as every note that reaches his ears tells him what words cannot say. He writes to capture the impalpable and offer an escape from time, where each sentence resonates like a suspended note.
Are there illustrations in the book?
The edition contains no illustrations to leave full room for the reader's imagination.
Can I get a signed edition?
Copies are periodically available during events. I also always keep a few in stock to give or sell if our paths happen to cross.
Is the book suitable for young readers?
The novel is a gay psychological romance (LGBT) and is recommended for readers aged 18 and over due to the depth of its themes and its maturity.
How can I support the author's work?
The best way to support Simon Lero is to leave a review on Amazon or Kobo after reading, and to share your favorite passages on social media with the hashtag #SimonLero.
How can I contact the author for an interview?
For any media requests, interviews, or collaborations, please use the contact form or visit the Media Space (Page under construction).
